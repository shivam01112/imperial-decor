About Us

About Us

IMPERIA DECOR is one of the fastest growing organizations in the interior & exterior infrastructure. It has been redefining the Indian Interior Exterior sector.

Established in 2013, IMPERIA DECOR is one of the leading manufacturers and exporters of Aluminium Composite Panels, Aluminium Composite Wall Panels and Aluminium Composite Roof Panels. We have total production capacity of 6 million Sq. Mtr. per annum.

Empowered with State-of-Art advanced technology hi-tech machines, 5 production lines & 16 LDP resin granules production machines under one roof which yields excellent results and ensures efficacy and products quality. IMPERIA DECOR Composite Panels is initiated to manufacture international standard Aluminium Composite Panels to meet the rising demand for quality building material.

Imperia Decor was established with the vision to provide exceptional aluminium fabrication services as the leading organization that achieved a remarkable position in the market with a sole focus on the quality of its products and services. IMPERIA DECOR is a name known for reliability and continuous improvement in the field of all types of aluminium composite panel manufacturing, structural glazing, and aluminium composite panel, shade and glass works, and more.

Our years of manufacturing experience include extensive corporate offices, banks, airline offices, hotels, recreational facilities like eateries, bistros and clubs, showrooms, all types of exclusive private spaces including farmhouses and landscaped areas, schools, assembly halls and so on.
