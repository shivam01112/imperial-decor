    Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities are strategically located in India, equipped with advanced machinery and modern production lines to ensure consistent quality and efficient delivery.

  • Do you provide customized panel solutions?

    Yes, we specialize in offering customized solutions in size, color, texture, and finish to meet unique architectural and design requirements for every project.

  • What certifications do your products carry?

    Our products are certified by leading national and international testing agencies for fire resistance, durability, and quality standards, ensuring long-term performance.

  • Do you export your products internationally?

    Yes, IMPERIA DECOR exports its products to several countries across Asia, the Middle East, Africa, and Europe, with a growing global client base.

    We use high-quality aluminium sheets, LDPE or FR-grade cores, and advanced coatings to ensure superior durability, weather resistance, and aesthetic appeal.

    Yes, we provide complete technical guidance, installation assistance, and training support to ensure flawless application of our products.

    Absolutely. Our panels are designed for versatility and can be used in both interior and exterior architectural applications with excellent weather resistance.

    With proper installation and maintenance, our panels have a lifespan of over 20 years, retaining their finish and structural integrity even in harsh climates.
