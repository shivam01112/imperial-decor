    IMPERIA DECOR is one of the fastest growing organizations in interior & exterior infrastructure, redefining the Indian Interior Exterior sector since 2013.

    We are leading manufacturers and exporters of Aluminium Composite Panels, Wall Panels, and Roof Panels, delivering top-quality materials for various construction needs.

    IMPERIA DECOR has a total production capacity of 6 million Sq. Mtr. per annum, supported by advanced hi-tech machines and 16 LDPE granule production units under one roof.
